# RDS-Business-ERP
## Overview

RDS ERP is a business management system being designed for stationery and printing businesses. The goal of the project is to simplify day-to-day operations such as inventory tracking, sales management, invoicing, supplier management, and customer record keeping through a single integrated platform.

This project originated from real business requirements observed in a stationery and printing store environment and is being developed as a practical software engineering and system design exercise.

## Project Goals

The primary objectives of RDS ERP are:

- Streamline inventory management
- Support barcode-based product lookup and billing
- Simplify sales and invoice generation
- Manage supplier and customer information
- Improve visibility into stock levels and - business operations
- Provide a foundation for future reporting and analytics features

## Current Status

The project is currently in the planning and design phase.</br>
Implementation and development phases will follow as the design continues to evolve.

## Vision
The long-term vision of RDS ERP is to evolve into a practical and scalable business management solution tailored for small and medium-sized retail businesses, beginning with the stationery and printing industry.
